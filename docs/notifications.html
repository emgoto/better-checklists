Notifications Settings

When a Checklist+ task is either approaching its due date or is due, Checklist+ can automatically post a comment on the card on your behalf, and @ mention the member assigned to the task.

Users with commenting enabled

Note: If a comment is posted on your behalf for a task assigned to you, you will not receive a notification. We recommend either making sure at least two board members have given commenting permissions, or creating a separate bot account just for this purpose.
