Notification Settings

When a task is either approaching its due date or is due, Checklist+ can post a comment on the card, and @ mention the member assigned to the task. This will send out a notification to that user, looking something like this:

Insert example iamge here!

To enable this feature, at least one user needs to have authenticated and given permission for comments to be posted on their behalf.

Please note that if a comment is posted on your behalf for a task assigned to you, you won't receive a notification. We recommend making sure at least two board members have given commenting permissions, or creating a separate bot account for this purpose.
